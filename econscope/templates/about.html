About

Melissa, the founder and author of econScope, is a full-time student who is passionate about global economics.

She was inspired to create econScope after delving into instructional economics and witnessing fiscal and monetary policy firsthand (namely from the COVID-19-induced recession). From keeping up with current events to analyzing different parts of the economy, econScope acts as an economic lens for a worldly outlook.

econScope is updated weekly to review changes in the economy from week to week. In addition, econScope provides analyses for select aspects of the economy. All econScope articles are recapitulations and analyses of real current events. Information and statistics are gathered from renowned news sources, all of which are either linked or listed as sources.

Outside of writing about economics, Melissa spends her time playing piano, bullet journaling, and baking. Enjoy exploring econScope!
