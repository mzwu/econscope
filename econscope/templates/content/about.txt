&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp	Covid-19, which stands for Coronavirus Disease 2019, came to light at the end of 2019 in Wuhan, China. Since then, the virus has spread to countries all around the globe and has amounted to more than 1.8 million cases worldwide. In addition to causing a global pandemic, the coronavirus has resulted in numerous impacts on the world’s economy. This site will share a high school student’s analysis of various economic outcomes in the United States of America, as well as personal opinions and experiences in the state of Virginia.
