WHAT:
Covid-19 is an infectious respiratory disease that is spread from person to person, caused primarily through respiratory droplets from coughing and sneezing. Symptoms range from mild to severe, commonly consisting of a fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. Some people with the infection are asymptomatic.
DO:
Wash your hands for 20 seconds with soap and water. If soap and water are unavailable, use a hand sanitizer containing at least 60% alcohol.
Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze. 
Avoid close contact (within 6 feet) with people who are sick.
Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household, especially if you feel sick.
Wear a face mask if you are sick, as recommended by the CDC.
Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily using common EPA-registered household disinfectants.
